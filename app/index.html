Living Light

“A lamp which harvests its energy through the photosynthetic process of the plant”



About

We create a world in which plants become part of our energy system and where technology and nature will merge.

‘Living Light’, a lamp which harvests its energy by the electrons released through bacteria in the soil during the photosynthetic process of the plant.

Softly stroking the leafs transforms this ordinary plant into a Living Light. The duration of light depends on the well-being of the plant. So the better you take care of the plant, the more energy you will receive back.
